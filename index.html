about me

I was born 30 April 1985, and I'm an Israeli actress and model.
At age 18, I was crowned Miss Israel 2004, Then I served two years in the Israel Defense Forces as a combat instructor, and began studying law and international relations at IDC Herzliya college while building up my modeling and acting careers.
My first international film role came as Gisele Yashar in Fast & Furious, a role I reprised in subsequent installments of the film franchise.
I went on to earn worldwide fame for portraying Wonder Woman in the DC Extended Universe, beginning with Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice (2016), followed by the solo film Wonder Woman and the ensemble Justice League (both 2017).
In 2018, I was included on Time's annual list of the 100 most influential people in the world.
